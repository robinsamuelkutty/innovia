About Us

The aspirant student group is an enthusiastic and diverse cohort, comprising both girls and boys who are first-year B.Tech students at CEP (College of Engineering Poonjar). With a shared passion for technology and a drive to excel in their chosen field, this dynamic group comes together to explore and expand their knowledge in various domains of computer engineering. They collaborate on projects, engage in discussions, and actively participate in workshops and competitions, aiming to foster an environment of learning and growth. With their combined talents and determination, this aspirant student group is poised to make significant contributions to the field of computer engineering and shape the future of technology.
